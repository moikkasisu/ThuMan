The Man Who Was Thursday

F: Hey, you know, you will always live in your past,

 ...forever on the road

M: Can't we just bury our past and move on?

F: I can, but you can't, you would not be able to.

(In silent darkness, she lighted another cigarette, slowly, white smoke rings swirled up into the air above them...)

“The air was soft, the stars so fine, the promise of every cobbled alley so great, that I thought I was in a dream.” She thought of this quote.
